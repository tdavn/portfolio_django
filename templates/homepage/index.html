As a "traditional" researcher for years, I later realized that I repeated myself. Day by day I did the same work while the world out there was changing so quickly.I couldn't remain dormant. I need to keep learning. I did think of computer science, think of my hobby of web building with Drupal CMS. Basically, I don't even have a CS degree. And nearly everything I learned about AI/ML and web development was all be myself. I'm my own personal teacher, with references from books, youtube on other internet sources. The more I have learn about Python, JavaScript, other languages and their scripts, the more surprised feeling I got. I am happy with this new start.
