About

Quō vādis?

How to deal with a life research problem as a whole? How to harness the power of AI/ML algorithms & big data to solve that problem? How to illustrate and display the solutions to others?

The life is a wonderful gift from God. To me, a cell is a universe. It contains mysteries from God to challenge human ambition. The cell, like a society, organize its molecules in a marvelous way to maintain its structures as well as functional implementations. I have experienced a long journey through exploring the nature of life, how a cell transform to a devil (carcinogenesis), how we manipulate a cell's genetic machinery (gene editing) ... "Whenever I think to already come closer a problem, foggier it is actually". One day, when searching data in Pubmed library, I suddenly realized that I need a new approach, a different way from milions milions here.

I am exploring to harness the power of computer plus AI/ML algorithms to uncover the lines through out bilions scientific works - the principles governing the life in the world of biological molecules.
